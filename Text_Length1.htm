Text Length

 

The following table lists the rules used to define the minimum and maximum length of the codes to be read.

Depending on the selected option, the scanner decodes:

 

  • One discrete-length barcode

  • Two discrete-length barcodes

  • Barcodes within a range of lengths

  • Any length of barcodes

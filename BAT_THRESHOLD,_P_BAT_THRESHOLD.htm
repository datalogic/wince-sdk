BAT_THRESHOLD, *P_BAT_THRESHOLD

 

The structure holds main battery threshold information.  

Please refer to Defined Values for information on BATTERY_SERIAL_NUMBER_SIZE and BATTERY_MFG_NAME_SIZE.

 

Members:

WORD low

Holds the battery capacity percentage in which the battery state should transitioned from high to low.

WORD critical

Holds the battery capacity percentage in which the battery state should transitioned from low to critical.
